My Story

My journey into AI began when I realized that technology wasn't just changing how we work — it was fundamentally reimagining what's possible. From my early days working with traditional machine learning models to now architecting enterprise-scale Generative AI solutions, every step has been driven by one question: "How can AI create real, meaningful value?"

At Hashedin by Deloitte, I work as a Python & GenAI Developer — designing and building LLM-powered applications, RAG systems, and AI agents that solve real business problems. From prototype to production, I focus on making Generative AI practical and impactful.

When I'm not coding, I'm writing here — breaking down complex AI concepts, sharing implementation patterns, and exploring what the future of human-AI collaboration looks like.
